# Design Foundation (Milestone 0)

## Visual direction

Nerd Cluster should feel cinematic, editorial, modern, and playful without clutter.

## Typography baseline

- Strong hero headline scale for editorial impact.
- Clear section heading hierarchy.
- Comfortable paragraph line-length and line-height.

## Spacing rhythm

- Consistent vertical section spacing.
- Predictable internal spacing for cards and content blocks.

## Color system

- Dark editorial base for cinematic tone.
- High-contrast foreground text.
- Distinct brand and accent colors for hierarchy and interaction.

## Accessibility baseline

- Preserve readable contrast between text and background.
- Provide a visible skip link for keyboard users.
- Maintain semantic heading order.

## Interaction baseline

- Keep interactions subtle and intentional.
- Avoid decorative motion with no UX purpose.
